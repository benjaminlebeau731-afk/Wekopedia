Wesley

Wesley is a highly documented, fast-moving entity known for his extreme physiological changes when subjected to anger. Under normal circumstances, Wesley appears relatively unremarkable; however, when provoked into a state of intense rage, his biological limits shift, allowing him to perform impossible feats of acceleration and violence.

Across various encounters and historical documentation, he has been referred to by a wide array of nicknames, including wesselsprout, weko nut, wekle, wekopedia, wetard, and simply wesley. Due to the rapid and lethal nature of his abilities, extreme caution is advised when engaging with him to avoid triggering an aggressive response.

Origins

The exact origins of Wesley remain a highly classified and hotly debated topic among Wekopedia scholars. Early records from late-1990s internet forums suggest he may have been the result of a failed government experiment designed to create the ultimate courier. Others theorize he is a naturally occurring anomaly, born from the collective rage of internet comment sections.

What is known for certain is that by the early 2010s, localized reports of a blur moving through urban environments and shouting "wekle!" began to surface, cementing his existence in urban legend before formal documentation began.

Abilities and Speed

The defining characteristic of Wesley is his conditional supersonic speed. When he becomes angry, a rapid physiological shift occurs within his central nervous system and muscular structure. This adrenaline-induced transformation allows him to run at speeds that breach the sound barrier (roughly 343 meters per second, or 767 miles per hour).

Observers have noted that as Wesley accelerates, a visible sonic boom and localized pressure waves often accompany his movement, shattering nearby glass and disrupting local air currents. Once the source of his anger is neutralized or he calms down, his speed reverts to typical human baseline levels.

Combat and Lethality

Wesley's combat capabilities are directly tied to his momentum. Because he can reach the speed of sound, he is able to attack and kill exceptionally fast. His strikes carry immense kinetic energy; a simple tackle or strike delivered at Mach 1 generates catastrophic force upon impact.

Tactical analyses of his attacks suggest that his reaction times are similarly heightened during his enraged state, making it nearly impossible for an average target to perceive or dodge his assault before it makes lethal contact. Defense strategies entirely revolve around pacification and avoiding the provocation of his anger in the first place.

Notable Incidents

Several catastrophic events have been directly attributed to Wesley entering a state of rage. The most well-documented is the "Great Server Crash of 2024". During a livestream, a user repeatedly spammed the phrase "weko nut" in a global chat. Seemingly sensing the provocation across the continent, Wesley accelerated to Mach 2.4, completely vaporizing the data center hosting the chat servers through sheer kinetic friction.

Another infamous event, known simply as the "Wetard Incident," occurred when a brazen detractor attempted to confront Wesley in an open field using the heavily derogatory nickname. The resulting sonic boom shattered windows in a 15-mile radius, and all that remained of the detractor was a smoking pair of shoes. It is because of this event that the term is strictly forbidden in most jurisdictions.

Etymology and Nicknames

Throughout his existence, communities and researchers have assigned a vast number of monikers to Wesley. The aliases vary from playful to derogatory, often reflecting the observer's relationship or prior encounters with him. The officially recognized catalog of his nicknames includes:

  • wesley – His baseline, default identifier.
  • wesselsprout – A diminutive or endearing alias.
  • weko nut – A colloquial term often used by those who have witnessed his chaotic bursts of speed.
  • wekle – A shortened, affectionate shorthand.
  • wekopedia – A portmanteau referencing the sheer volume of lore and information surrounding his mythos (and the namesake of this very encyclopedia).
  • wetard – A heavily derogatory and controversial nickname used by his most brazen detractors, usually right before triggering his lethal, supersonic rage.
